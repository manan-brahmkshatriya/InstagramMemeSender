# Background Music for Reels

Drop royalty-free instrumental/motivational music files here.
Supported formats: `.mp3`, `.m4a`, `.aac`, `.wav`, `.ogg`

The daemon picks one randomly per reel and mixes it in at **18% volume**
so the quote text remains the star. The music loops/trims to match the
video length automatically.

## Where to get free tracks (no attribution required)

1. **Pixabay Music** → https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Filter by mood: Motivational / Uplifting / Inspiring / Cinematic
   - All tracks are 100% free, no attribution needed

2. **Mixkit** → https://mixkit.co/free-stock-music/
   - Category: Inspirational · Corporate · Upbeat
   - Free for commercial use, no attribution

3. **Free Music Archive** → https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Filter by license: CC0 or CC BY for most permissive use

## Recommended vibe for @riseclub9

- Instrumental / no lyrics
- Uplifting, cinematic, or lo-fi beats
- BPM 80-120 works well under motivational reels
- Aim for 5-10 tracks so the bot has variety and doesn't repeat too often
